BağçaTap Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

BağçaTap helps parents find kindergarten information such as address, contact details, prices, age groups, photos, and map location.

Information We Collect

  • Location: If you press the nearby/location feature and allow permission, the app uses your device location to show nearby kindergartens. Location is used for this feature and is not used to personally identify you.
  • App usage statistics: The app may create an anonymous device ID to count how many devices opened the app and how many times kindergarten profiles were viewed.
  • Notifications: The app may use notification permission to show BağçaTap updates sent by the administrator.
  • Admin uploaded content: Kindergarten photos and information uploaded by the administrator are stored so they can be shown in the app.

How We Use Information

  • To show kindergartens near the user when location permission is granted.
  • To improve the app and understand which kindergarten profiles are viewed most.
  • To display kindergarten information, uploaded images, and administrator notifications.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. The app uses map and hosting services to display maps, pages, images, and app content.

Data Retention

Anonymous usage statistics and kindergarten content may be kept while the BağçaTap service is active. Admin uploaded images can be removed by the administrator when no longer needed.

User Choices

  • You can deny location permission and still browse kindergarten information manually.
  • You can disable app notifications in Android settings.

Children

BağçaTap is intended for parents and adults looking for kindergarten information. It is not designed as a child-directed app.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the BağçaTap administrator through the app listing contact email in Google Play.
